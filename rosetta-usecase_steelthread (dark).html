Executive Summary
Turn our highest-value customers into lifelong Northern Trail advocates — at scale, in real time.

Northern Trail Outfitters has built a loyal base of outdoor enthusiasts since 2017. Our top two customer tiers represent a disproportionate share of revenue, yet today we interact with them the same way we do every other shopper — batch emails, generic promotions, disconnected in-store and online journeys. We are leaving material revenue on the table.

This initiative funds the deployment of an AI agent and unified data layer to identify, personalise for, and continuously re-engage our highest-value customers across every channel — creating measurably higher basket sizes, repeat purchase rates, and lifetime value.

"The brands that win the next decade will not merely know who their best customers are — they will anticipate what those customers need next, before the customer thinks to ask."

The Problem We Are Solving
Our data is fragmented. Our best customers can't tell.

A Tier 1 customer who bought a Gore-Tex jacket in-store last winter, browsed hiking boots online in March, and contacted support about a return in April exists in four separate systems — our POS (two different systems), Agentforce Commerce, Agentforce Marketing, and Agentforce Service — with no unified identity. We cannot see them as one person, so we cannot treat them as one person.

The result: our VIP customers receive the same mass-market communications as first-time buyers, experience friction when they cross channels, and feel no recognition of their loyalty. They have every reason to buy from a more personalised competitor.
