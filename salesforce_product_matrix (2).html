⚠ Key Structural Issues

🔥 DATA360 is a Fiction

The Agentforce 360 page presents Data 360 as an umbrella for Informatica, Tableau, and MuleSoft. Clicking the link goes only to Data Cloud. Trailhead also uses "Data 360" as the product name, not "Data Cloud". 3+ active names for the same product.

🔥 $10 Challenge: Agentforce vs Agentforce 360 vs Salesforce AI

No consistent definition across pages. Homepage: "Salesforce brings together Slack, Agentforce, Customer 360, and Data 360" — Agentforce 360 doesn't appear here. Now Trailhead adds "Agentforce 360 Platform" as a product category, different from "Agentforce" and different from "Salesforce Platform" on the pricing page.

⚠ 4 Different Service Nav Menus

salesforce.com main nav, salesforce.com Agentforce 360 page, Trailhead, and the homepage image all show different sub-items and different names for the same Service products. "Self-Service" vs "Service Portal" vs "Agentforce IT Service" across properties.

⚠ 17 Industries in Nav, 16 Pricing Blocks, 13 in Trailhead

Each property has a different count and different grouping. Trailhead's industry list only goes to 13 (no Automotive, no Travel/Hospitality/Transport, no Construction/Real Estate, no Tech, no Professional Services). Healthcare + Life Sciences merged on pricing page. Different splits create customer confusion about what exists.

🔵 Trailhead: The Only Place With the Full "Agentforce" Rebrand

Trailhead has implemented what appears to be Salesforce's intended future naming: "Agentforce Health", "Agentforce Financial Services", "Agentforce Commerce", etc. But this naming is used nowhere else in the entire Salesforce ecosystem — creating maximum inconsistency during a transition period.

ℹ Quip: $750M Acquisition — Invisible on Salesforce

Quip (acquired 2016, ~$750M) has its own site (quip.com) with pricing and a dev portal. Completely absent from salesforce.com nav, pricing page, developer, admin, architect sites, AND Trailhead's product dropdown. Not a single Salesforce property with a nav lists it.

ℹ Heroku: Parallel Developer Ecosystem

Heroku operates devcenter.heroku.com as a completely separate developer ecosystem. Not integrated into developer.salesforce.com. Trailhead's product dropdown includes Heroku under "Agentforce 360 Platform" — at least one property groups it correctly.

◈ MuleSoft AI Products Invisible on salesforce.com

Agent Fabric, Agentforce MuleSoft, MuleSoft Vibes — all on mulesoft.com and Trailhead, none on salesforce.com's main nav or pricing page. Trailhead includes "Agentforce MuleSoft" and "MuleSoft Anypoint Platform" but not the newer AI products.
